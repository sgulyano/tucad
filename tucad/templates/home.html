Chest X-Ray Computer-Aided Diagnosis

This project aims to help radiologists by pre-screening the patients. This will help patients get their treatments when they need it and radiologists can concentrate on patients with severe conditions first and be able to save more lives.

Caution

This software cannot replace radiologist's diagnosis. It can only give the second opinion and it is designed as a means to get a quick diagnosis.
